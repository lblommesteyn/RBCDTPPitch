THE PROBLEM

Through our research:

More than 235,000 people in Canada experience homelessness in any given year

and 25,000 to 35,000 people may be experiencing homelessness on any given night.

London Homeless Coalition revealed 27 people experiencing homelessness have died this year.

Leading us to our problem statement of:

How might we raise awareness about the specific needs of food banks and inspire/encorage non-perishable donations from everyday Londoners?

WHAT WE BUILT

Offerit

Offerit is a mobile application that allows Londoners both interested and able, to help less fortunate indviduals affected by homelessness through volunteering and donating
